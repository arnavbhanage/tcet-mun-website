Group Discussion

The First Step Towards Leadership

The Group Discussion serves as the gateway to TCET MUN. Participants demonstrate their communication skills, analytical thinking, teamwork and confidence while discussing contemporary issues.

  • Critical Thinking

The Flagship Conference

Represent countries, debate international issues, draft resolutions, negotiate with delegates and experience diplomacy in action over three days of engaging committee sessions.

  • Committee Sessions
  • Diplomacy & Negotiation

  • Democratic Values
